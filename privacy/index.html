Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 14, 2025

This Privacy Policy describes how Super Raffle ("we", "our", or "us") collects, uses, and shares your information when you use our mobile application Super Raffle ("the App").

1. Information We Collect

1.1 Personal Information

We do not directly collect personally identifiable information (such as name, email, address) from you through the App. All raffle data and participant details you enter are stored locally on your device and are not transmitted to our servers.

1.2 Usage Data

We may automatically collect certain anonymous data when you use the App, such as:

  • Device type and operating system
  • App version and usage statistics
  • Crash logs and diagnostics
  • General location data (not precise GPS)

This is done via third-party services such as Google Analytics for Firebase to help us improve the App and fix bugs.

1.3 Cookies & Tracking Technologies

The App itself does not use cookies. However, third-party ad networks may use cookies, device identifiers, or similar technologies to personalize ads displayed within the App.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the data we collect to:

  • Operate and maintain the App
  • Improve performance and user experience
  • Analyze usage to fix bugs and optimize features
  • Serve personalized or non-personalized ads (if applicable)

3. Legal Basis for Processing (GDPR)

If you are in the European Economic Area (EEA), our legal basis for collecting and using information is:

  • Legitimate Interests: To analyze and improve our App.
  • Consent: Where required for personalized ads or analytics, we will obtain your consent.
  • Compliance with Legal Obligations: To comply with applicable laws.

4. Sharing of Information

We may share anonymous or aggregated data with:

  • Analytics providers (like Google Analytics for Firebase)
  • Advertising partners for delivering ads

We do not sell your personal data.

5. International Transfers

Your information may be processed outside of your country of residence (for example, in the United States), which may have different data protection laws. We take steps to ensure appropriate safeguards are in place.

6. Data Retention

We retain usage data only as long as necessary for analysis and to comply with legal obligations. Local data you store in the App remains on your device until you delete the App or clear its data.

7. Your Rights

Depending on your location, you may have rights under data protection laws, including:

  • Access to the data we hold about you
  • Correction of inaccurate data
  • Erasure of your data
  • Restriction or objection to processing
  • Data portability

To exercise these rights, please contact us at support@superraffleapp.com.

California Privacy Rights (CCPA)

If you are a California resident, you may request to know what personal information we have collected, request deletion, or opt-out of the sale of personal information (note: we do not sell personal data).

8. Children's Privacy

We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe we have accidentally collected such data, please contact us so we can delete it.

9. Security

We use reasonable administrative, technical, and physical safeguards to protect your data. However, no security measures are perfect, so we cannot guarantee absolute security.

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with a new "Last updated" date. We encourage you to review it periodically.

11. Contact Us

If you have any questions or concerns about this Privacy Policy, please contact us:

  • Email: support@superraffleapp.com

Thank you for trusting Super Raffle!
